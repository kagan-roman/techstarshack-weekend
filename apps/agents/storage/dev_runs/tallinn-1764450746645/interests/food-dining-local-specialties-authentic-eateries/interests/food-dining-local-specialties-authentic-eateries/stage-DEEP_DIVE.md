## Locations Found (only if exceptional)
- Balti Jaama Turg (Baltic Station Market)
  - Why unique: Central market hub blending classic Estonian produce (smoked fish, blood sausage, black bread) with 25+ street food corners; key to Tallinn’s everyday food culture.
  - Hours (to validate seasonal): Mon–Sat 9:00–20:00; Sun 9:00–18:00.
  - Source: https://astri.ee/bjt/en/avaleht/ and IG @baltijaamaturg
  - touristTrap estimate: 45
- Maiasmokk Café & Marzipan Room
  - Why unique: Estonia’s oldest café (1864) where marzipan figurines are hand-painted; historic Viennese-style interior.
  - Hours: typically daily ~9:00–21:00; verify winter hours.
  - Source: https://maiasmokk.ee/en/marzipan/
  - touristTrap estimate: 60
- Nõmme Turg
  - Why unique: Historic suburban market favored by locals for smoked meats/fish, sauerkraut, and honey; fewer tourists than Old Town.
  - Hours: Mon–Fri 8:00–19:00; Sat 8:00–17:00; Sun 9:00–16:00 (to validate)
  - Source: https://nommeturg.ee/
  - touristTrap estimate: 35
